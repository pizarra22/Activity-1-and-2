FAQ

Admissions

Q: What are the requirements for admission?

Applicants need to submit their high school diploma, transcript of records, and a completed application form. Entrance exams are also required.

Programs

Q: What courses does MSC offer? MSC offers a variety of programs including Bachelor's, Master's, and Doctorate degrees in fields such as Education, Business, and Engineering.

Tuition and Fees

Q: Is MSC covered by the Free Tuition Law?

A: Yes, MSC is included under the "Universal Access to Quality Tertiary Education" law, so tuition is free for eligible students.

Scholarships

Q: Are there scholarships available?

A: Yes, MSC offers several scholarship programs for qualified students.
